Strawberry Milkshake Recipe

Strawberry Milkshake is a refreshing energy drink to recharge your energy levels. Good for kids as well as breakfast beverage.

If you get sweet strawberries then its best as we dont need to add any sugar. Otherwise add sugar as required. You can serve the milkshake plain or with a scoop of vanilla ice-cream or strawberry ice-cream and have a strawberry milkshake

INGRIDIENTS

  • Strawberries

  • Milk

  • Sugar

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Rinse the strawberries and drain them

  2. Slice off green leafy part. This is also called hulling. Then chop the strawberries.

  3. Add chopped strawberries to a blender jar and add 2 cups of milk.

  4. add 3 tabspn of sugar or as required. If strawberries are sweet, no need to add sugar.

  5. Pour in glass and serve.
