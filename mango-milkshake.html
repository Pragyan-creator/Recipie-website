Mango Milkshake Recipe

Mango shake is a very popular Indian summer drink during mango season.

This mango shake recipe makes for a delicious and creamy shake. It quenches the thirst, keeps one full and is nutritious too. Mango shake also helps in Weight gain

This recipe is made with ripe sweet mangoes and whole milk.Do note that manoes should be sweet not semi-sweet or tangy.

Ingredients

  • Mangoes

  • Milk

  • 2-3 ice cubes(optional)

  • 2 tablespoons sugar or honey or jaggery (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse and pat dry two medium-sized mangoes. Peel and chop them. Add chopped mangoes in a blender jar.

  2. Add 1.5 cups milk

  3. Also add 2-3 ice-cubes

  4. Blend till smooth

  5. Pour in glasses

  6. Serve shake immediately
